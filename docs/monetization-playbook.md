# Monetization Playbook - doeon-kim-portfolio

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** Portfolio-to-lead router
- **Primary buyer:** Recruiters, founders, enterprise AI leaders, and technical buyers.
- **Revenue wedge:** A productized gallery that routes viewers into flagship demos and commercial proof lanes.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Consulting lead-gen site** - pricing hypothesis: Not sold directly
2. **Recruiting proof surface** - pricing hypothesis: $3k-$15k advisory entry project
3. **Partnership demo router** - pricing hypothesis: $8k-$30k pilot from flagship lane

## Acquisition Channels

- GitHub Pages
- Pinned LinkedIn profile
- QR link in resumes and decks

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- npm run verify passes
- All repo links are current
- Coverage ledger maps active works

## Risk Boundaries

- Avoid exposing private repos
- Keep claims evidence-backed
- Do not overstate customer traction

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Add 3 flagship walkthrough videos
- **60 days:** Create consulting CTA
- **90 days:** Track inbound by lane

## Metrics That Matter

- Demo clicks
- Recruiter conversion
- Pilot inquiries

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
